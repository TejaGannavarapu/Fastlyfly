UV GULLAS college of medicine

UV GULLAS college of medicine was Established In 1977 Which Is Recognised By Ched-Philippines, Medical Council Of India, Who, WDOMS, ECFMG-USMLE, FAIMER-IMED & Medical Boards Of USA & UK. In Addition To The Vicente Gullas Hospital, UV Gullas Is Affiliated With Four More Hospitals In CEBU. one of Top Medical College in Philippines in Cebu city. International students have the oppertunity to study medicine in phillipines at affordable cost and world class University. The college has successful alumni who have achieved well in the fields of law, business, politics, academe, medicine, sports and other endeavors. At University of the Visayas, we prepare students for a global competition.The college is on the top of the priority list for education from abroad. Every year, thousands of students around the globe come to the UV Gullas College of Medicine to pursue their goal of becoming a well established doctor. Situated in the Cebu City of the Philippines, the UV Gullas Medical College offers the best medical education to international students at an affordable tuition fee. The Philippines Medical College has been performing outstanding amongst most other colleges in the country. Students who have graduated from the UV Gullas College of Medicine have a higher success rate in the USMLE exam in the US and FMGE exams in India. Students from India, especially south India come to the Philippines for higher studies. The facilities provided at the UV Gullas College of Medicine attract Indian students a lot. As the college already have an impressive number of Indian students. The college also provides Indian food and has a welcoming gesture for the Indian students.

Applying for admission

MBBS In Philippines Admission Procedure

  • Step1: Fill out the College Admission Application form.
  • Step2: Submit photocopies of Your Passport, HSC Mark Sheet to apply for a Letter of Permission for Admission.
  • Step3: Receive the confirmation greeting letter from the medical college.
  • Step4: After confirmation, apply for the Philippines Visa along with all the required documents.
  • Step5: Appear for Personal Visa Interview at Philippines Embassy alongside the greeting letter and other essential documents for Visa Approval.

  • 50% marks in 10+2 norm with physics, chemistry, and biology or equivalent advanced education.
