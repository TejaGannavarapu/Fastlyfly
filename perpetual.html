About Us

Perpetual University Foundation

The University of Perpetual Help System is located in Dalta, Las Piñas Philippines. It is a catholic based university, with campuses in Las Piñas, Bacoor, Cavite, and Calamba City, Laguna in the Philippines. Perpetual Help College of Rizal (PCHR) was founded on Feb 5th 1975 by Dr Gen. Antonio Laperal Tamayo and Dr Daisy Moran Tamayo. Initially, PCHR offered only one degree, Bachelor of Nursing, in which 700 students enrolled. It established its Molino and Calamba campuses in 1995 and 1996, respectively. Finally, in 1997, it received its university status from the Commission of Higher Education (CHED). In 2017, CHED conferred upon the university its autonomous status.

Applying for admission

MBBS In Philippines Admission Procedure

  • Step1: Fill out the College Admission Application form.
  • Step2: Submit photocopies of Your Passport, HSC Mark Sheet to apply for a Letter of Permission for Admission.
  • Step3: Receive the confirmation greeting letter from the medical college.
  • Step4: After confirmation, apply for the Philippines Visa along with all the required documents.
  • Step5: Appear for Personal Visa Interview at Philippines Embassy alongside the greeting letter and other essential documents for Visa Approval.
